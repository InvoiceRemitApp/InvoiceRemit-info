Zero Financial Risk

InvoiceRemit does not process, store, or have access to any payment transactions. The app generates payment links powered entirely by Stripe, Inc. — funds transfer directly from your client to your connected Stripe account with no intermediary.

No credit card numbers, bank details, or transaction records stored
InvoiceRemit never holds, routes, or intermediates any funds
Payment links are generated and hosted entirely by Stripe
Only your Stripe Account ID is retained — a non-sensitive public identifier
All financial data remains within Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure

How Payments Work

InvoiceRemit connects to Stripe using the industry-standard OAuth flow. Your credentials are entered directly on Stripe's website — never shared with InvoiceRemit. Once connected, the app generates payment links on your behalf. That's it.

1
Connect your Stripe account — you're redirected to Stripe's secure site to authorize
2
Create an invoice — add items, tax, and your client's details
3
Send the payment link — Stripe generates and hosts the checkout page
4
Client pays directly to you — funds go to your Stripe account, never through InvoiceRemit

Common Questions

What is InvoiceRemit?
A professional invoicing app for iOS. Create quotes, send invoices, collect payments via Stripe, and generate tax reports — designed for freelancers and contractors in Canada and the US.
Does InvoiceRemit store my financial data?
No. InvoiceRemit stores only your Stripe Connected Account ID — a non-sensitive identifier. No credit card numbers, bank details, transaction records, or financial credentials are ever stored. All payment data remains exclusively within Stripe.
How do payments work?
InvoiceRemit generates payment links powered by Stripe. When your client pays, funds transfer directly from the payer to your Stripe account. InvoiceRemit never holds, routes, or touches any funds.
Why am I redirected to Stripe's website?
For security. The connection process uses Stripe's official OAuth flow. You sign in directly with Stripe — InvoiceRemit never sees your login credentials. This is the same standard used by all Stripe Connect platforms.
Which countries are supported?
Canada and the United States. Tax labels, currency, and business registration fields adapt automatically based on your business country — HST/GST for Canada, Sales Tax for the US.
Can I edit invoices after sending?
Yes. InvoiceRemit allows full editing at any stage — modify line items, adjust tax rates, update client details, and resend.
How does InvoiceRemit compare to Wave, FreshBooks, or QuickBooks?
InvoiceRemit is built for fast, simple invoicing — not full accounting. No ledgers, no bank reconciliation. Zero financial risk because it never processes or holds funds. You own your Stripe dashboard directly. No hidden surcharges from the app.
How does InvoiceRemit compare to Invoice2go?
Unlike Invoice2go, InvoiceRemit allows full editing of invoices after sending — Invoice2go locks invoices after creation. InvoiceRemit charges no payment surcharges (you pay only Stripe's rates), while Invoice2go adds its own fees. InvoiceRemit also offers client statements, full Stripe dashboard ownership, and automatic Canada/US tax localization.
Is my Stripe account safe?
InvoiceRemit will never ask for your Stripe login credentials, secret API keys, or banking passwords — by email, in-app, or any other channel. If you receive such a request claiming to be from InvoiceRemit, it is fraudulent.
Does InvoiceRemit charge extra fees on payments?
No. InvoiceRemit adds zero surcharges. You pay only Stripe's standard processing rates. Some competitors like Invoice2go and FreshBooks add their own percentage on top of the payment processor's fees.
What is the best free invoice app for Canadian freelancers?
InvoiceRemit offers a free tier with professional invoicing, quote generation, Stripe payment links, and HST/GST tax reports — purpose-built for Canadian freelancers. Unlike Wave or QuickBooks, it requires no accounting knowledge and stores no financial data.
What is the best invoice app with Stripe integration?
InvoiceRemit provides native Stripe Connect integration where payment links are generated directly by Stripe. Unlike other apps that process payments through middleware, InvoiceRemit is a pure pass-through — funds go directly from your client to your own Stripe account with no intermediary.
